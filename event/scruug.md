# Stop Scruug!

## SzHb's Notes

### Goodies

1. Jolly Stockings
   - T5/9/10. 1.725x luck when Godforged
   - From (Arisen) (Ebon) Scruug
   - The only leg piece with luck bonus
2. Arisen North Star
   - Off-hand with Mana Burn II
   - From Arisen Ebon Scruug
   - For quick Iconoclast
3. Presents!
   - T5 luck accessory
   - From Scruug
   - The best luck accessory before you get Unfelled Concord.
